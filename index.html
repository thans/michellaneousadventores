Departure

Seattle to Bellingham | June 15, 2015

We departed Seattle around 7:00 PM and caught a ride with my parents on their way back to Bellingham from a wedding in California.

In Bellingham, we were greeted by my grandma Nanny Kenny visiting from Newfoundland, and my uncle Fabe visiting from Victoria. We enjoyed a beautiful sunset with some Strawberry Shortcake. We also got to witness Nanny Kenny demo her new e-cigarette she is trying out to help her quit smoking! Go Nan!

- Mitchell
