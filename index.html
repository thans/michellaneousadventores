Go Blue Jays!

Toronto | June 17, 2015

Some big highlights from today included lunch at Caplansky's Deli (we didn't have to wait until NY for the Deli experience), a trip to the Hockey Hall of Fame, and a 7-1 Blue Jays victory over the Mets!

The Hockey Hall of Fame is a must visit for hockey fans in Toronto. In addition to jerseys from every player inducted into the hall of fame, there was a trophy room with the original Stanley Cup and a replica of the current cup. They even had a hands on exhibit for you to try shooting at video-game goalie projected on a screen - or you could be the goalie and attempt to block shots from a video-game offense which flung pucks out of the wall at you! Sadly, Mitch and I got to this exhibit at closing time, but we were able to sneak in a period of NHL 2015 on the PlayStations they had.

- Tore(onto)
